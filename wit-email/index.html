Thank you everyone who helped us at our Gala!
Last Friday's Dream Big gala brought out the fantasies of everyone who attended. There were wildly imaginative costumes, awesome moves on the dance floor, and tons of laughter!

Thanks to the generosity of our community, the event grossed more than $34,000, with the silent auction breaking records in terms of revenue and the number of items up for bid.
Project Create Classes Start for Spring
With support from the National Endowment for the Arts, WIT recently launched two Level 1 classes at Anacostia's Project Create with 23 students.This is the third year of adult classes we've done at Project Create.
Musical Conservatory Students Graduate
The first cohort of 35 improvisers some of whom are pictured here to graduate from WIT's Musical Improv Conservatory. Now they will go on to conquer the world of musical improv and bring peace to the world.
More Ensemble Members Added to Our Ranks
Our family of performers continues to grow! WIT added 21 new players to our Harold program. We've also added members to Uncle Gorgeous and Hellcat, bringing our number of improvisers into the thousands.
