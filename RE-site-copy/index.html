About Me

Hello, I'm Aung Min, a real estate photographer from Queens, NYC. I specialize in taking photos that highlight the best parts of each property and help potential buyers see themselves living there. I've photographed all kinds of homes, from small apartments to big townhouses. I know real estate moves fast, so I'm always ready to work around your schedule and get you the photos quickly.

Why Professional Pictures?

Great photos are the heart of real estate listings, but photo editing is the key to truly make them shine. When you invest in professional photo editing services for your real estate images, you're taking your visual presentation to the next level.
